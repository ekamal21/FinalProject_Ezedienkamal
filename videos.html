Parrots, also known as psittacines, are birds of the roughly
398 species in 92 genera comprising the order Psittaciformes,
found mostly in tropical and subtropical regions. The order is
subdivided into three superfamilies: the Psittacoidea, the
Cacatuoidea, and the Strigopoidea
Most parrots live in tropical areas. Parrots have curved bills
(beaks), strong legs and clawed feet.
